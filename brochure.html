Our Services

We provide your loved one with the utmost virtual comfort. Should you choose to sign up with us, you will be offered from a selection of tiers based on the payment method of your choosing.

If you can manage to pay up front, we can ensure they will provided with multiple rooms with multiple purposes, as well as a simulation of basic human needs. The tier below is paid by subscription. For the month, your loved one will be guaranteed two rooms and an outdoor area. The rooms included are a bedroom and a living room (others not included as this tier does not include simulation of needs). At the lowest tier, which is a one-time payment for every visit, your loved one will be granted a bedroom with lovely amenities, including a window! Don't worry, the real magic comes from YOUR visits.
